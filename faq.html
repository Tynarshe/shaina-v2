Before you book

Ask a Question
What areas do you serve?

We provide cleaning services from our Sheffield base, including residential, commercial, office, deep, carpet and window cleaning enquiries.

How do you price your services?

Pricing is based on the size of the space, condition, cleaning frequency, scope of work and any specialist services requested. We provide estimates before confirming a booking.

Are your products safe for pets and children?

We can use environmentally friendly product choices suitable for homes with children and pets. Please mention any sensitivities or preferences when booking.

Can I book a one-off clean?

Yes. One-off cleans are available for deep cleaning, move-in or move-out needs, pre-event preparation and seasonal refreshes.

More support

Common booking details.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

Our team can bring professional cleaning supplies. If you prefer specific products for your property, let us know before the visit.

Can you clean outside normal office hours?

We review timing requests case by case, especially for commercial spaces that require low-disruption cleaning windows.

What should I do before the cleaner arrives?

Please secure valuables, provide access instructions and tell us about priority areas, fragile items or surfaces requiring specific care.

Can I request the same cleaner for recurring visits?

Where scheduling allows, we aim to maintain consistency for recurring clients so the cleaner understands your space and preferences.

Do you handle end-of-tenancy cleaning?

Deep cleaning and move-out style services can be arranged. Share the property size, condition and deadline when requesting a quote.

How quickly will you reply?

We aim to provide a quick response within 24 hours for online enquiries.

Still deciding?

Tell us about your space and we will guide you.
